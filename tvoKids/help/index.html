STEPS
TO TAKE

Don't reply to or share cyberbullying messages with others.

Save any messages or posts where cyberbullying happens, including the date and time they were sent. Take screenshots or print them out, and show them to a grown-up you trust.

You can also block the person who is cyberbullying you, so they can't send you any more messages.
